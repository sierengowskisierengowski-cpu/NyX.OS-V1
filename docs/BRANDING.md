---

## Background Treatment

The NyX.x.OS background is never flat black. It has:
- Organic ink-wash white patches scattered irregularly
- Each patch different size, opacity, and shape
- No geometric forms, all edges broken and natural
- Panels sit on top with slight transparency so texture shows through
- Visual reference: aged black fabric, charcoal on paper, sun-faded plastic, ink wash art

---

## Border Treatment

Panel and component borders contain:
- Sparse binary code fragments, short sequences
- Randomly positioned, never evenly spaced or patterned
- Color #5B3D6A with subtle glow effect
- Glow is noticed, not announced
- Both horizontal and vertical placements allowed

---

## Typography

- Font: JetBrains Mono Nerd Font everywhere, no exceptions
- Certain key words use accent colors mid-string
- Uppercase for panel titles and labels
- Letter-spacing 2-3px on labels and titles

## What NyX.x.OS Is NOT

- Not neon cyberpunk
- Not rainbow colored
- Not glossy or glassy
- Not symmetric or clean-patterned
- Not loud

It is heavy, aged, functional, and dark. Every design decision serves that.
